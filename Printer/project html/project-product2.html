HP Tango X Printer

$399.99

Product Information

HP's Tango X smart home printer delivers convenient, effortless printing that's controlled almost exclusively from the HP Smart app on your iPhone or iPad. Just plug it in, download the app, and you're ready to go. No USB connection or drivers required. The compact design includes an indigo linen textile-finish wrap that doubles as an output tray to save space.

System Information

Operating System: OS X version 10.11 or later, Windows 10, 8.1, 8, 7
Requirements Other: 1.5GB free hard drive space
